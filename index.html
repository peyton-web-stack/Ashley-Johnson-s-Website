Meet The Maker ♡

What started as a simple creative hobby quickly turned into something I truly fell in love with. I’ve always been drawn to unique accessories and beautiful details, and I wanted to create earrings that felt lightweight, stylish, and easy to wear for everyday life. After spending time experimenting with different styles and techniques, I discovered a passion for creating handmade pieces with a modern boho and coastal-inspired feel.

As a one-woman small business, every single pair is handmade by me with care and attention to detail, from designing and creating to packaging your order. I work with materials such as polymer clay, a variety of metal findings, and UV resin accents in select designs to create pieces that feel unique, fun, and easy to style.

More than anything, I want Skye & Lane Co. to feel personal, welcoming, and genuine. Whether you’re shopping for a statement piece or an everyday favorite, I hope you find something that makes you feel confident and beautiful when you wear it.

Jewelry Care

To help your jewelry stay beautiful for as long as possible, please follow these care tips:

  • Avoid contact with water, perfumes, lotions, oils, and harsh chemicals.
  • Remove jewelry/Accesories before showering, swimming, exercising, or sleeping.
  • Store pieces in a dry, cool place away from direct sunlight and humidity.
  • Handle with care, polymer clay and handmade details can be delicate if dropped or bent with force.
  • Gently clean with a soft, dry cloth if needed.
  • Keep jewelry/Accesories away from makeup, hairspray, and other beauty products to help preserve their finish.

Each piece is handmade with care using materials such as polymer clay, UV resin, and a variety of metal findings. Small variations are part of what makes every piece unique.

With proper care, your jewelry can stay beautiful for years to come.
